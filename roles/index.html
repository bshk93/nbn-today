Roles & Permissions

What each access level can do in the NBN system.

Admin

Admin

Full system access. There is one admin token. Admins can do everything the Rosters role can do, plus:

  • Create, list, and revoke access tokens for other members
BOD

Board of Directors

All Rosters permissions, plus early access to award results before they're publicly revealed.

  • Everything the Rosters role can do
  • Preview unrevealed award season results (other members see a "not released yet" message)
Rosters

Rosters

Manages all roster and contract operations across the league.

  • Record transactions — free agency signings, draft picks, option decisions
  • Edit any team's active roster (players, OVR ratings)
  • Edit any team's draft pick ownership, protections, and swap rights
  • Edit player bio data — salary, cap holds, contract type, personal info
  • Add new players to the player database
  • Delete transaction records from the log
  • Delete draft pick rows
Curator

Curator

A subset of Rosters. For members who maintain player data but do not handle contracts.

  • Edit OVR ratings (current and history) for any player
  • Edit non-contract player bio fields — name, positions, DOB, college, country, draft info, photo, height, weight, wingspan, jersey number

Curators cannot record transactions, edit contracts or salaries, edit rosters or picks, or add new players.

Stats

Stats

For members who enter game data and maintain the stats database.

  • Access the Box Score Import tool to parse and commit game box scores from screenshots

Stats does not grant roster editing, transaction recording, or player bio access.

Team Roles

Team Roles (atl, bkn, bos, …)

One role per NBA franchise. Assigned to a team's front office members.

  • Update that team's trade block — add, remove, or annotate players available for trade
  • Assign jersey numbers for players on that team's roster

Team roles do not grant roster editing access. Members who also manage contracts need the Rosters role separately.
