How trade validation works

The simulator and the league's transaction system run the same rubric: the page calls POST /api/validate/trade, which shares its validator with POST /api/transactions. A "legal" result here is what the office will accept. Picks are tracked but carry no cap or roster weight.

Salary matching — max incoming salary by team type

  • Below the 1st Apron (§ 4.2) — tiered on the salary you send out:
    • outgoing ≤ $8,527,000 → max incoming = 2 × outgoing + $250,000
    • $8,527,000 < outgoing ≤ $29,000,000 → max incoming = outgoing + $8,527,000
    • outgoing > $29,000,000 → max incoming = 1.25 × outgoing + $250,000
  • At/above the 1st Apron (§ 4.3) — no tiers: max incoming = outgoing + $250,000.
  • At/above the 2nd Apron (§ 4.4) — same outgoing + $250,000 limit, and the team may not aggregate (combine two or more outgoing salaries) to match incoming. Flagged as a warning for the office to verify.

A team's tier is set by its salary before the trade. Incoming ≤ outgoing always matches.

MLE trade absorption (§ 4.2a)

Pick an exception under a team's selector to absorb that team's incoming salary against its remaining season MLE balance instead of matching outgoing salary — outgoing sent back doesn't matter under this path. Eligibility mirrors free-agent use of the same exception: NTMLE is blocked at/above the 1st Apron, TMLE at/above the 2nd Apron, and the Room Exception requires Team Salary more than a full NTMLE below the Cap. Using the NTMLE or TMLE this way triggers the same hard-cap-at-apron effect as a signing.

Other checks

  • Hard cap — a team hard-capped at an apron (per its team-state) can't exceed that apron after the trade; a league-wide hard cap, if set, applies to everyone.
  • Roster size (Article II) — 15 standard players in-season; the offseason ceiling is 20. Ending at 16–20 is a warning (trim to 15 before the season); above 20 is a hard block.
  • Leg count (§ 4.1) — a normal trade has two or more legs. Single-sided legs are allowed (e.g. a draft-show split) but flagged.
